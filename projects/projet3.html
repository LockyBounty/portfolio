Private Bank App Prototype

A Prototype of Mobile Application developed for Delen Private Bank during a hackathon of 2 days in Antwerpen (Belgium). Work in a team of 3.

This game has been developed as an extra feature for a book, and it is multi platforms, you can play it on a web browser, Iphone and Android.
Developed under Godot Engine.
